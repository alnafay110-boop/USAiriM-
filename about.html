Our Story

About USAiriM Publishing

USAiriM Publishing is dedicated to producing authentic Islamic books, journals, planners and educational resources based on the Qur'an and the authentic Sunnah. Our mission is to help Muslims — and anyone curious about Islam — grow in beneficial knowledge and apply it in everyday life through clear, well-researched digital publications.

Our editorial standard

Every title we publish is checked against the Qur'an and hadith graded sahih or hasan by recognized scholars, so readers can trust what they're reading rather than second-guess it. We write in clear, modern English so the content is accessible to new Muslims and lifelong students of the deen alike.

Who we're for

USAiriM Publishing serves English-speaking readers in the USA, UK, Europe and beyond who want practical, faith-rooted guidance — whether that's building daily habits, understanding the Seerah, or simply reading something beneficial before bed.

✍️

From the founder

USAiriM Publishing is founded and written by an Islamic Studies lecturer and Khateeb, drawing on years of teaching and delivering khutbahs to bring authentic, well-sourced Islamic content to a wider, English-reading audience.
