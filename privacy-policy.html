Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Information we collect

When you contact us or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name and email address. When you purchase a book, checkout is handled entirely by our payment processor, Payhip — we do not receive or store your card details.

How we use your information

We use your email to send order confirmations, respond to support requests, and — only if you opted in — send occasional updates about new releases. We do not sell or rent your information to third parties.

Third-party services

Purchases are processed by Payhip, subject to Payhip's own privacy policy. Our contact and newsletter forms are processed by FormSubmit. We recommend reviewing their policies for details on how they handle your data.

Analytics and cookies

We use Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use this website — for example, which pages are viewed, how long visitors stay, and general navigation patterns such as clicks and scrolling. These tools use cookies and similar technologies and may collect information such as your approximate location, device and browser type, and pages visited. This data is aggregated and used only to improve our website and content; it is not used to personally identify you. You can control or block cookies through your browser settings, and you can learn more about how these providers handle data in Google's Privacy Policy and Microsoft Clarity's terms.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email, or by contacting usairim132@gmail.com.
